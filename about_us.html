About Us

Welcome to Black-Lily, where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of New Delhi our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts and connoisseurs alike.
At Black-Lily, we are passionate about creating unforgettable dining experiences. Our talented chefs meticulously craft each dish with the finest ingredients, blending flavors and textures to create culinary masterpieces that tantalize the taste buds.
But it's not just about the food; it's about the atmosphere and the people. Our cozy, inviting ambiance and friendly staff ensure that every visit is a memorable one. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply craving a delightful meal, we are here to exceed your expectations.
We take pride in our commitment to sourcing local and sustainable ingredients, as we believe in supporting our community and preserving the environment. Join us at Black-Lily for a journey of flavors, where every plate tells a story. We look forward to serving you and making your dining experience exceptional.

About Our Restaurant

"Welcome to Black-Lily where the flavors of India come to life on your plate. Our passion for authentic Indian cuisine is evident in every dish we prepare. From the rich aroma of our spices to the vibrant colors of our curries, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey through India right here in South West New Delhi Our chefs craft each dish with care, using traditional recipes passed down through generations, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience that captures the essence of India's diverse culinary heritage. Whether you're a seasoned spice enthusiast or new to Indian cuisine, our menu offers something for everyone. Join us and savor the true taste of India.

Our chefs

"At Black-Lily our culinary magic is orchestrated by a team of passionate and skilled chefs who are the heart and soul of our kitchen. Hailing from various regions of India, our chefs bring a wealth of experience and expertise in crafting authentic Indian dishes that tantalize your taste buds. We invite you to savor the passion and skill of our chefs as they take you on a gastronomic adventure you won't forget."
